Modern Solutions Shaping Infrastructure Performance

Infrastructure project managers, engineers, and contractors are under increasing pressure to reduce maintenance costs, increase service life, improve performance, and complete projects on tighter schedules.

At CAMX, you'll find the materials, technologies, and expertise helping infrastructure professionals meet those demands. Discover solutions shaping every corner of infrastructure, including:

Transportation

Fiber-reinforced polymer (FRP) rebars and structural shells that can replace steel, preventing internal rust and concrete degradation.

Woven carbon-fiber and fiberglass fabrics for wrapping existing columns to dramatically improve earthquake flexibility and column ductility.

Water Treatment and Sanitation

Advanced inner coatings that prevent mineral scale buildup, keeping water friction low and reducing electricity needs at pumping stations.

UV-curable fiberglass liners that can be pulled through broken sewers and inflated, forming a new pipe inside the old one without ripping up roads.

Energy and Power

Carbon-fiber-core conductors which sag less when hot and carry up to twice the current and are able to replace traditional steel-core power lines.

Carbon-fiber wraps for high-pressure hydrogen storage tanks to withstand pressures up to 700 bar without leaking or degrading.

Telecommunications

Specialized fiberglass composites for 5G antennas that protect the electronics while allowing high-frequency signals to pass without interference.

Graphene-based materials for conducting heat away from processors quickly within data centers, lowering cooling bills.

At CAMX, discover solutions like these, connect with industry experts, and explore how advanced materials are shaping the future of infrastructure.
