Where Infrastructure Needs and Composite Solutions Converge

CAMX—The Composites and Advanced Materials Expo, is the largest event of its kind in North America, bringing together manufacturers, engineers, fabricators, suppliers, and infrastructure professionals together in one place to explore the latest innovations and practical solutions shaping today's infrastructure projects.

How Composites Are Shaping Modern Infrastructure

New composite materials are shaping the future of infrastructure. At CAMX, explore long-lasting, cost-efficient composite alternatives to traditional materials. Whether your projects require improved durability, corrosion resistance, lighter-weight structures, or longer service life, CAMX provides direct access to practical solutions ready for implementation.
